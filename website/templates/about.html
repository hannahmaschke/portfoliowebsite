About Me

Hi, I'm Hannah Maschke.

I'm a software developer based in the Durham Region, just east of Toronto. My passion is turning complex challenges into elegant, user-friendly software solutions. I have a background in full-stack development, I enjoy working on all aspects of a project, from designing a robust backend architecture to building a professional looking and responsive front-end.

My journey into tech was driven by a desire to build things and solve problems. I write clean code and design appealing user interfaces. I find it very rewarding bringing an idea to life. In my free time I enjoy developing personal projects including games and useful web applications. I am always learning new technologies and studying new courses online that will add to my software development knowledge. I develop a wide range of software, including both web, cloud, and desktop applications. I am open to employment opportunities, including freelance web development and full time employment as a software developer.
